Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Digital Sanctuary ("the App") is a motivational quote application developed by Sudarshan Awasthi. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

Digital Sanctuary is designed with your privacy in mind. The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

  • No account required — You can use the App without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Local storage only — Your favorite quotes and cached data are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No analytics — We do not track your usage, behavior, or interactions within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles this data.
  • GitHub (Content Delivery) — Quote content is fetched from a publicly hosted file. No user data is sent during this process.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Cached quotes (for offline access)
  • Your favorite quotes
  • App preferences

This data is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App contains no user-generated content, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
